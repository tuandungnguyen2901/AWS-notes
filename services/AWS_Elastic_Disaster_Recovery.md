# AWS Elastic Disaster Recovery (DRS)

**Primary Use**: Rapid, reliable disaster recovery

**Key Features**:
- Pilot Light strategy
- Continuous block-level replication
- Cross-Region recovery
- Multi-account recovery
- Automated failover
- Failback to on-premises
- Point-in-time recovery
- EBS volume replication
- TCP ports 443 (control) and 1500 (data)
- TLS encryption

**Mentioned In**: Disaster Recovery, Backup and Recovery Strategies
